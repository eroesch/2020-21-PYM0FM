fMRI Analysis: Introduction

In the course of the coming weeks, we dive into the analysis of BOLD fMRI data, and take our first steps with FSL. I will often use and refer to concepts that you are also reviewing in PYM0S1 Statistics, concurrently to this module, as both modules are very complementary; please keep an eye out, and do ask questions in both modules.

We will take a "flipped" approach, whereby I will recommend videos for you to watch at your own pace, from the FSL training course, and we will spend our time together on practical examples. We will not cover all 42 videos of the course, which span quite advanced topics. Instead, we will focus on a selection of videos, which I will also complement with the odd video from Youtube.

For your reference, you can find all the material for the course material https://fsl.fmrib.ox.ac.uk/fslcourse/online_materials.html, and the accompanying videos on Youtube (https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLvgasosJnUVl_bt8VbERUyCLU93OG31h_).

Watch:

  • Principles of fMRI, by Martin Lundquist and Tor Wager: Video 1 (9 min), and Video 2 (13 min)
  • 11. FMRI1: Introduction to task fMRI experiments and analysis (fMRI1 E1), by the FSL team:
    https://youtu.be/y7wtbMl3y4E — 14 min
  • 22. FMRI3: Pipeline Overview (fMRI3 E1), by the FSL team:
    https://youtu.be/aAbUDr1FqG4 — 15 min

Must read:

  • Chapter 1 of Poldrack, R., Mumford, J., Nichols, T. (2011). Handbook of Functional MRI Data Analysis. Cambridge: Cambridge University Press. doi:10.1017/CBO9780511895029. Accessible from the library there.

Should read:

  • Lindquist, M. A. (2008). The Statistical Analysis of fMRI Data. Statistical Science, 23(4), 439–464. https://doi.org/10.1214/09-STS282
  • Smith, S. M. (2004). Overview of fMRI analysis. The British Journal of Radiology, 77(suppl_2), S167–S175. https://doi.org/10/cmmp4k
  • Soares, J. M., Magalhães, R., Moreira, P. S., Sousa, A., Ganz, E., Sampaio, A., Alves, V., Marques, P., Sousa, N. (2016). A Hitchhiker’s Guide to Functional Magnetic Resonance Imaging. Frontiers in Neuroscience, 10. https://doi.org/10/gfztc4
